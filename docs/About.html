Testonics is a testing and automation service provider that addresses a wide variety of testing challenges. We are a team of testing professionals with different backgrounds who aspire to lead the fast developing automation world.

Our purpose is to make automation testing easy and accessible for our clients by bringing in our experience and expertise and designing the best architecture to fit their needs.

We write our frameworks with different employee capabilities in mind, so that a team of manual testers would be able to fit into the project at different stages with different levels of involvement. We pride ourselves in taking clients to a whole new level of automated testing.
